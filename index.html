Effortless, Error-free Writing

Produce flawless writing with stet's AI-powered editing program

Canadian Press Style Proofreader

Every Canadian writer spends hours on editing their pieces to align with the Canadian Press style. With stet, we give back valuable time to writers and ensure a flawless edit. Canadian writers will be able to have their own pocket-editor through all platforms: mobile, desktop, and tablet.

Available Wherever, Whenever

Edit on the go or at home. stet is available for all Canadian writers on desktop, mobile, or tablet. Available as a Google chrome and Safari extension, as well as a mobile and tablet keyboard.

You will be able to use stet for emails, messages, online documents, and social media.

stet is Made for Writers, By Writers

Improve Journal Writing

Writers often start off with a journal. This program can give back valuable time, which can be used to create more content.

Explanation of CP Style Rules

Not only will stet fix CP style errors, but it will also allow for an explanation. Understanding that some writers may not want explanations, this feature with stet is optional.

Learn Better

Students will be able to use this editing program to their advantage and learn from their mistakes. stet allows students to grow and teaches them why the change is necessary.

Save Time

Every writer has gotten that feeling when they need to quickly write their thoughts, send out a press release, or finish a story on a tight deadline. Instead of spending hours (or precious minutes), stet gives journalists more time to fact-check stories, reach out to sources, and meet tight deadlines.

A Second Pair of Eyes

stet acts as a pocket editor on the go. From the mobile keyboard to a computer keyboard and tablet, stet allows journalists and other writers to edit any document effortlessly.

Focus on Story Accuracy

editors often spend many hours on fixing CP style errors. With stet, the program will be able to ensure correct style writing, allowing editors to focus on fact-checking and accuracy on stories.

Ensure Consistency

stet is an automated program that automatically follows the Canadian Press style. Many news organizations or non-profit organizations may want to capitalize or do something against the CP style guidelines. stet allows for flexibility and will create a dictionary tailored to your organization to ensure writing consistency.
